This project is a simple participatory website that collects pairs of things people care about. I was interested in the fact that care is never singular or stable, and that the combination of two concerns can often feel more honest, strange, or funny than one alone. Each submission is stored in a database and appears on the site, creating an evolving public archive of attachments, questions, and unresolved concerns. This archive treats care as something collective, layered, and sometimes unexpectedly absurd to look at all together.
